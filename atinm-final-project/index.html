Welcome to the MonoMuse Museum

MonoMuse has been inspiring visitors since 2016 through cutting-edge exhibitions and interactive experiences.

From our humble beginnings with a single digital storytelling exhibition, MonoMuse has grown into a world-class institution featuring over 20 rotating exhibitions, an interactive media lab, and international artist collaborations. Our team of curators and technologists work together to bring you experiences that blur the line between art and innovation.
