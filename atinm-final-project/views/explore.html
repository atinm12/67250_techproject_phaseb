Mission Statement

MonoMuse serves to preserve the history of artistic innovation. We exhibit the progression of tools used by artists, from prehistoric paintbrushes to modern-day technology. When visiting MonoMuse, we hope our visitors are inspired by how innovations can revolutionize the way humans express ideas through art.

Target Visitors

  1. Artists
  2. History enthusiasts
  3. Anyone who enjoys learning!
